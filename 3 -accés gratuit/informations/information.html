Psychological Disorders Information

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)

Symptoms:

  • Unwanted thoughts
  • Repetitive behaviors or mental acts
Treatment: Cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), medication (if necessary).

Depression

Symptoms:

  • Persistent sadness
  • Loss of interest or pleasure
  • Feelings of hopelessness
Treatment: Therapy, medication, lifestyle changes.

Anxiety Disorders

Symptoms:

  • Excessive worry
  • Fear
  • Avoidance behaviors
Treatment: Therapy, medication (if necessary).

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

Symptoms:

  • Flashbacks
  • Nightmares
  • Hyperarousal
Treatment: Psychotherapy, medication (if necessary), support groups.
